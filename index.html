Privacy Policy MemojoFind

Effective date: 23 October 2025
Developer / Publisher: Memojo, by APMC
Contact: frank@memojo.eu

Summary

  • We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.
  • All photo analysis and search (NLP) happen entirely on your device. device.
  • The app makes no network requests for your content and uses no third-party analytics/ads SDKs.
  • You can revoke photo access at any time in iOS Settings.

What this app does

Memojo lets you search photos in your device’s native gallery using natural language. To do this, the app reads photo metadata and content on your device and builds a local index that never leaves your device.

Data we process

  • Photos & videos (and their metadata): processed locally to power search. On-device index: a searchable index stored only on your device.

We do not:

  • collect or transmit your photos, metadata, search queries, contacts, location, identifiers, or diagnostics to our servers;
  • sell data;
  • use third-party trackers or ads SDKs.

Data sharing & transfers

  • None. We do not send your data to anyone. No international transfers occur because no data leaves your device.

Analytics

  • We do not collect analytics in the app and we do not use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • Apple may provide us aggregated and anonymous metrics in App Store Connect—such as downloads, sessions, crashes, and app deletions—from users who have chosen to share diagnostics with developers at the system level. These reports do not identify you and do not contain your content.

Legal basis (GDPR)

We do not act as a controller of your personal data because we do not collect it. Any processing of your photos is a local device not collect it. Any processing of your photos is a local device operation under your control and for your personal/household purposes. If you contact us (e.g., support email), we will process that message solely to respond, then delete it within a reasonable period.

Your choices and controls

  • Revoke Photos access: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Memojo.
  • Delete the app: removes the local index and app data from your device.

Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. The app contains no user accounts and no data collection.

Security

  • All processing is on-device.
  • No cloud databases, no external servers.

Permissions we request (iOS)

  • Photos: to read photos/videos solely for on-device indexing and search.
    • iOS usage string suggestion: “We access your photo library to search your photos on this device. Nothing leaves your device.”

(No tracking permission (ATT) is requested. No background network access for analytics or ads.)

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and the “Effective date.” Substantial changes that introduce data collection will require your consent and an updated App Privacy label.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email frank@memojo.eu Legal Address: Chaussee de Waterloo 19, 1060 Belgium

App Privacy “Nutrition Label” (for App Store Connect)

  • Data Used to Track You: None
  • Data Linked to You: None
  • Data Not Linked to You: None
  • Collected via the App: No data collected(All photo processing and indexes remain on-device.)
